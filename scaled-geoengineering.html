Proposal

Maybe there should be a Stratospheric aerosol injection (SAI), venture backed, for-profit startup on Australian soil.

Legality

SAI is legal in the United States under the Weather Modification Act of 1976 makesunsets2024faq.

Theory

Unlike the United States, Australia is a party to the United Nations Convention on Biological Diversity, which includes an agreement to restrict climate geoengineering unless it is for small-scale scientific research, or unless the risks and scientific basis are appropriately considered (Decision X/33, 2010).bowen2024hansard
