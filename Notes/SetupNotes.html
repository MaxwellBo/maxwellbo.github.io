Truthfully, I use all four for different purposes. I use Vim as a quick and dirty text editor for the manipulation of files in primarily commandline workspace. Atom has helpful features for web-development and an insanely high level of customization, but is slow, laggy and takes an age to respond. Sublime has great Markdown support and boots instantly for quick and easy text manipulation.
